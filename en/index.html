Dr. Pedro Cruz Dinis

He began his career in Medical Emergency at INEM. He went through Aesthetic Medicine, but his passion for Hair Restoration was greater.

Born in Angola, he has lived on 4 continents, never stopping traveling, always seeking to expand and share his knowledge in trichology and hair restoration surgery. He is also a member of several associations worldwide.

Of his 30 years of experience as a doctor, 15 years have been dedicated to changing lives and boosting self-esteem because that's how he feels fulfilled!

Journey

  • Began practicing hair transplants in 2007.
  • First Hair Transplant Course using the FUT technique in 2007 under the guidance of plastic surgeon Dr. Zoran Lekic.
  • Specialization in Hair Restoration using FUT, FUE, and BHT techniques in Bangkok, Thailand (2011), under the guidance of Dr. Viroj Vong.
  • Hundreds of Hair Restorations performed using FUE, FUT, and BHT techniques.
  • Graduated in Medicine from the Faculty of Medicine of Lisbon in 1994.
  • Specialist by the Portuguese Medical Association in Occupational Medicine.
  • Courses in Aesthetic Medicine.
  • Multiple courses in Medical Emergency.
  • Postgraduate in Senior Technician of Occupational Health and Safety.

Treatments

Learn more about our treatments.

PRP

PRP - Platelet Rich Plasma

Treatment that uses the patient's own blood to combat hair loss and strengthen hair. The platelet-rich plasma is isolated and injected into the scalp. This process releases growth factors that activate follicles, improve circulation, and stimulate hair growth.

Hair Mesotherapy

Treatment that uses microinjections of vitamins, minerals, and amino acids into the scalp to nourish follicles, strengthen hair, and stimulate hair growth. It is indicated to reduce hair loss and improve hair density and health.

Medication

Medications for hair loss, such as minoxidil, finasteride, spironolactone, and alphaestradiol, promote healthy hair growth and slow hair loss, as well as nourish the scalp and stimulate blood circulation.

Laser Hair Therapy

Laser hair therapy uses low-intensity light to stimulate circulation in the scalp and activate hair follicles. It helps reduce hair loss, strengthen hair, and improve hair density. It is painless, safe, and can be used as a complement to other hair treatments.
